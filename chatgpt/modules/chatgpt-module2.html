Module 2: Prompt Engineering Mastery

Lesson 1: What is Prompt Engineering?

Prompt Engineering is the skill of writing clear and effective instructions for AI tools like ChatGPT to get better results. A good prompt helps AI understand your goal, requirements and expected output.

Lesson 2: What is a Prompt?

A prompt is any question, command or instruction that you give to ChatGPT.

Example:

Simple Prompt: "Write an article about AI."

Better Prompt: "Act as a professional content writer and write a 1000-word beginner-friendly article about Artificial Intelligence with headings and examples."

Lesson 3: Parts of a Professional Prompt

  • Role: Tell ChatGPT who it should act as.
    Example: Act as an SEO expert.
  • Task: Explain what you want.
    Example: Create a keyword research plan.
  • Context: Give background information.
    Example: My website is about AI education.
  • Format: Tell the output style.
    Example: Create a table or bullet points.
  • Goal: Explain the final objective.

Lesson 4: Types of Prompts

  • Creative Writing Prompts
  • Learning Prompts
  • Coding Prompts
  • SEO Prompts
  • Marketing Prompts
  • Business Prompts

Lesson 5: Prompt Improvement Technique

If the answer is not perfect, improve your prompt by adding more details, examples and instructions.

Example:

First Prompt: "Create a logo idea."

Improved Prompt: "Create 5 modern logo ideas for an AI learning website. The style should be professional, simple and suitable for students."

Lesson 6: Common Prompt Mistakes

  • Giving unclear instructions
  • Not providing enough information
  • Not mentioning the target audience
  • Not asking for a specific format
  • Accepting the first answer without improvement

Professional Prompt Formula

Role + Task + Context + Format + Goal
