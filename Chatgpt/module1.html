📖 Lesson 1 - What is Artificial Intelligence?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a technology that enables computers to think, learn and solve problems like humans. Today AI is used in education, healthcare, business, banking, marketing and software development.

📖 Lesson 2 - What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is an AI assistant developed by OpenAI. It understands human language and helps users with writing, coding, learning, research, translation and much more.

📖 Lesson 3 - OpenAI

OpenAI is an Artificial Intelligence research company. Its goal is to build safe and useful AI tools for everyone.
