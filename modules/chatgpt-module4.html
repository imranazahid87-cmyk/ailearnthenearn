Module 4: ChatGPT for SEO & Digital Marketing

Lesson 1: What is SEO?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the process of improving a website so that it ranks higher in search engines like Google. Higher rankings bring more visitors and increase online visibility.

Lesson 2: How ChatGPT Helps in SEO

  • Keyword Research Ideas
  • SEO-Friendly Titles
  • Meta Descriptions
  • Blog Outlines
  • FAQ Generation
  • Content Optimization
  • Internal Linking Ideas
  • Schema Markup Assistance

Lesson 3: Keyword Research

Keywords are the words people type into Google when searching for information. ChatGPT can help generate keyword ideas based on your topic and target audience.

Example Prompt

"Act as an SEO expert and generate 50 low-competition keywords for an AI education website."

Lesson 4: Writing SEO-Friendly Content

A high-quality article should include:

  • Keyword-rich Title
  • Clear Headings (H1, H2, H3)
  • Short Paragraphs
  • Helpful Information
  • Frequently Asked Questions
  • Strong Conclusion

Lesson 5: ChatGPT for Digital Marketing

ChatGPT can support digital marketing by helping you create:

  • Facebook Posts
  • Instagram Captions
  • LinkedIn Content
  • Email Campaigns
  • Marketing Strategies
  • Advertising Ideas

Lesson 6: Creating a Content Calendar

Plan your content in advance by asking ChatGPT to generate weekly or monthly content ideas for your website or business.

Example Prompt

"Create a 30-day content calendar for an AI learning website."

Lesson 7: SEO Best Practices

  • Write original content
  • Focus on user intent
  • Use relevant keywords naturally
  • Create helpful and trustworthy articles
  • Update old content regularly
  • Optimize images and headings
