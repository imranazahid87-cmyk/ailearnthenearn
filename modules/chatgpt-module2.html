ChatGPT Mastery - Module 2

Prompt Engineering Basics

In this module you will learn how to write effective prompts to get better results from ChatGPT.

Lesson 1: What is a Prompt?

A prompt is a question or instruction that you give to ChatGPT. A clear prompt helps AI understand your requirements better.

Lesson 2: How to Write Better Prompts

  • Give clear instructions
  • Add context and details
  • Tell AI the desired format
  • Review and improve results
