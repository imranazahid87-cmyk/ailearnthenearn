🎯 What Is SEO?

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. It is the process of improving a website so search engines like Google can understand its content and rank it higher in search results. Good SEO helps websites receive more visitors without paying for advertisements. Instead of buying traffic, SEO focuses on creating valuable content, improving website quality, and giving users the best experience.

Example: If someone searches "Best Free SEO Course" Google will try to show the most useful and trustworthy pages first.
